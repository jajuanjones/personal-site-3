About Me

Jajuan is a mostly self-taught software developer based out of the music city, Nashville, Tennessee. In the year 2019, Jajuan was exposed to computer programming through online mediums like YouTube and Reddit. At first he enjoyed the comedic aspects of the software development community but slowly developed a curiousity for programming. Eventually, enough curiosity built up for him to attempt to learn himself. After a long and arduous educational journey, Jajuan officially became a Software Developer in 2022. Currently, Jajuan moreso enjoys the front-end side of the stack, however, he is most practiced in Python, Python being his favorite langauge. Jajuan is able to develop scalable full-stack applications, but is currently on a journey to become more specialized in one area.
